Meet the Team

Project Manager & Mechanical Lead

Electrical Lead

My primary responsibilities was the communication from the User interface side to the lower level electronics, working on controlling the belt as well as codesigning our central PCB. In my free time I love playing basketball, hiking, and going to coffee shops.
