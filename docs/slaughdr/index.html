About Our Project

Vlog Anywhere

A Serialized, Lightweight, Arial Unit Generating High Definition Recording, our vlogging drone is designed to be hands off and easy to use. Powered by a 22.2V battery and controlled by a Nvidia Jetson, you can use this drone to record in a variety of enviornments. Try it today.
